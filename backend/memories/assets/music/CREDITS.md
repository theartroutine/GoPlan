# Music credits

Attribution for bundled memory-video tracks.

- **CC0 / Public Domain tracks**: no attribution required — leave them off this list.
- **CC-BY tracks**: attribution is REQUIRED. It is recorded here AND shown to end
  users in the app (music picker, in-app viewer) and on the public share page,
  driven by the `license` fields in `music_catalog.py`.
